# Glossary

- **Player**: Customer who leases a real plot and interacts through the application.
- **Plot**: Real physical growing area assigned to one active lease.
- **Lease**: Agreement that grants a player use of a plot under defined rules.
- **Crop Cycle**: Lifecycle of one crop planting on one plot.
- **Digital Twin**: Digital representation of the real plot and its current state.
- **Player Action Request**: A player's requested action, subject to policy evaluation.
- **Action Policy**: Rule set that decides whether and how a request may proceed.
- **Automation Command**: Validated command sent to a real actuator.
- **Work Order**: Scheduled manual farm task.
- **Work Evidence**: Media or data proving task completion.
- **Care Log**: Append-oriented history of agricultural actions.
- **Incident**: Event that threatens crop, safety, service, or entitlement.
- **Replacement Assignment**: Transparent transfer to a replacement plot/crop after an incident.
- **Harvest Batch**: Measured produce collected from a crop cycle.
- **Harvest Entitlement**: Quantity and ownership record assigned to a player.
- **Traceability Record**: Public-safe history linked to QR.
- **Simulator**: Software that behaves like the real gateway and devices.
- **Watchdog**: Safety mechanism that stops an actuator after configured limits.
